# **MMSx Authority Institute**
### *501(c)(3) Nonprofit Organization (United States)*  
### *The Global Authority in Movement Mechanics & Biomechanics Research*

The **MMSx Authority Institute** is an international nonprofit research organization dedicated to raising the global standards of movement mechanics, biomechanics, neuromechanics, and professional exercise-science education.

Representing experts and applied researchers across **50+ countries**, we develop validated frameworks, clinical protocols, and movement intelligence systems that define the new gold standard in human movement analysis and performance.

---

## 🌎 **Our Mission**
To establish a unified, science-backed, and globally accessible system for movement assessment, professional education, injury reduction, and performance optimization.

We combine:

- Biomechanics  
- Neuromechanics  
- Movement science  
- Clinical assessment  
- Functional training  
- Artificial intelligence  
- Real-world applications  

into a single integrated research ecosystem.

## 🧬 **Core Scientific Frameworks**
Our DOI-registered, globally recognized systems include:

### **• NEEBAL™ Principle**
