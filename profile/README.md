## About MMSx Authority Institute

MMSx Authority Institute is a **U.S.-registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit scientific institute** dedicated to the development of movement mechanics as a structured decision-science discipline. We advance biomechanics through registered research, open datasets, peer-reviewed publication, and global standards development.

> **Our core position:** Human movement is governed by force vectors, torque regulation, moment-arm dynamics, and load transfer. Clinical and performance decisions must be based on measurable mechanical variables — not observational posture alone.

We operate at the intersection of:

| Domain | Focus |
|---|---|
| Biomechanics | Force-vector analysis, torque regulation, load distribution |
| Neuromechanics | Neuromuscular timing, sequencing, and control |
| Clinical Science | Load management, rehabilitation translation, injury reduction |
| Performance Science | Strength mechanics, sport biomechanics, RFD optimization |
| Applied Systems | Movement intelligence tools, AI-supported assessment platforms |

## Core Frameworks

<table>
<tr>

### MMSx-SCAN™
Multi-domain biomechanical assessment system. Evaluates movement across 7+1 analytical dimensions. n=870 inter-rater reliability study. **Peer-reviewed.**

[![DOI](https://img.shields.io/badge/DOI-10.66078%2FJMMBS-blue?style=flat-square)](https://doi.org/10.66078/JMMBS.2026.V3I1.016)

### MOVE™ Protocol
Movement-Oriented Velocity of Engagement. 4-phase, 8-week clinical rehabilitation framework. Multi-site interventional study. **Peer-reviewed.**

[![NCT](https://img.shields.io/badge/NCT-07220200-16a34a?style=flat-square)](https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT07220200)

</td>

### BPIT™ 5-Line
Balanced Progressive Intensity Training. Force-distribution and progression framework for movement correction and strength development. **Peer-reviewed.**

[![NCT](https://img.shields.io/badge/NCT-07296640-16a34a?style=flat-square)](https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT07296640)

</td>

Neural-Energy-Efficiency-Biomechanics-Alignment-Load. Multi-variable internal regulation model integrating neural, elastic, respiratory, and load-distribution dynamics.

## Governance & Scientific Integrity

Research operates under documented ethics protocols with full transparency:

- **STROBE** — observational study reporting
- **CONSORT** — interventional trial reporting
- **GCP ICH E6(R2)** — good clinical practice
- **Declaration of Helsinki** (2013) — participant protection
- **IMSO S101** — movement intelligence assessment standard
- **Pre-registered SAP** — statistical analysis plan locked before data collection
