# MMSx Authority Institute  
### Global Research Institute for Movement Mechanics & Biomechanics

The **MMSx Authority Institute** is a U.S.–based 501(c)(3) nonprofit research organization advancing global standards in:

- Biomechanics  
- Neuromechanics  
- Movement Science  
- Clinical Movement Assessment  
- Evidence-Based Training Systems  
- Multisite Clinical Trials  

We are the creators of several internationally recognized frameworks:

- **NEEBAL™ Principle**  
- **BPIT™ – Balanced Progressive Intensity Training**  
- **MOVE™ Protocol** (NCT07220200)  
- **MMSx-SCAN™ Clinical Assessment System**  

Our mission is to establish globally unified, evidence-backed movement science education and research for professionals, institutions, and sports bodies worldwide.
